Sms Parkavimas App Privacy Policy

Sms Parkavimas does not collect, process or transmit any personal data with the exception of the provided vehicle's registration plate number, parking zone and parking duration. This information is sent via an SMS message to your Mobile Service Provider's phone number 1332, where it is processed and the parking fee is included in your mobile phone bill. If you have any further questions about how this information is handled, please contact your mobile service provider.
